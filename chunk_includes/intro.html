Welcome to the survey Sentence Evaluation!

In this survey, you will read different sentences in Spanish and answer questions related to them.

To participate in this survey, you need to:

  • Speak Spanish at B1 level or above;
  • Be at least 18 years old;
  • Complete this survey on a laptop or a computer (not on a mobile device), and in full screen.

The survey will take about 20 min to complete. There is no option to go back once clicking next.

There will be more detailed instructions and a practice sentence.
